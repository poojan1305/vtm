OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

Founded in 1996 by Mr. Paras V. Mehta, Mr. Suresh V. Mehta, and Mr. Chetan V. Mehta, C E Infra (India) has rapidly emerged as one of Mumbai's fastest-growing construction firms.

Dedicated to serving major Municipal Corporations and Government bodies, our focus is on creating nationally significant infrastructure assets.

At C E Infra (India), our vision extends beyond the horizon as we aspire to be a pioneering force in the construction industry. Established in 1996 by the visionary trio of Mr. Paras V. Mehta, Mr. Suresh V. Mehta, and Mr. Chetan V. Mehta, our journey is marked by a steadfast commitment to excellence

Our overarching vision is to play a pivotal role in transformative construction, creating infrastructure assets of national significance. We aim to be recognized as a dynamic and innovative construction firm that not only meets but exceeds the expectations of major Municipal Corporations and Government bodies.

In our early years, we began as a humble construction firm, gradually evolving into one of Mumbai's fastest-growing entities. Through the years, our dedication to delivering top-notch construction services garnered the trust of major Municipal Corporations and Government bodies.

Since 1996, C E Infra (India) has been a pioneering force, evolving into a leading construction firm, shaping the landscape of Mumbai with a legacy of excellence..
